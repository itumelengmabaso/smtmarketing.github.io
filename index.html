About Us

What inspired the establishment of the company?

South Africa is currently facing a problem of unemployment which keeps on rising on an alarming rate, faces the inability of industries that also fail to create jobs and mostly for the upcoming generation/youth. The government has by far tried to establish programmes that will help industries to try and eradicate unemployment rate and also enforced opportunities to the youth of today to try and pursue opening businesses and also create their own wealth.

The inspiration behind the establishment of SMT MARKETING is not only about job creation but will want to give small and medium- sized enterprises the platform they deserve in the marketing world. The company strives to give recognition of different types of upcoming and newly developed companies.

The South African economy and Africa as a whole will only be successful if the majority race that is poor is empowered and given the stage and the opportunity to participate in the economy and to be given their equal roles in the economies activities. In this case the company will ensure that black owned companies get the exposure they need to grow their business and also increase their capital and communicate better with their potential customers. The company ideal goal is to give companies the ability to attract more customers by not only showing off their work to the public and social media but also strive to grow each and every business that will fall under our marketing strategies.
